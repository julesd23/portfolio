About Me

Hello everyone my name is Julius DeAngelis. I’m a South Carolina native, and I’m also a little bit of a multifaceted individual to put it plainly. I have had a long career in music and drumming, I’ve always been enamored with technology, and I’ve been passed down a passion for cooking through my father as well. Throughout the recent past I’ve toured the US and Canada with multiple musical acts, I’ve worked on film sets doing production assistant work, and I’ve even become a sous chef at a popular restaurant in Charleston, South Carolina. Nowadays I’m pursuing something I feel like I should have a while ago, technology and software. This February I enrolled in MIT Xpro’s Full Stack development program to gain a solid foundation in software development, and I’m looking forward to learning and growing in this field for many years to come.
